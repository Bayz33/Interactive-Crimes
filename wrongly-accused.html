Case 5: Wrongly Accused

A man sits behind bars for a murder he didn’t commit—or so he claims. As new evidence emerges, it’s up to you to reopen the case, sort truth from lies, and bring justice where the system failed.

Your mission: Reinvestigate the case and answer three key questions:

  • Who actually committed the crime?
  • What was their motive?
  • What key piece of evidence proves it?

Witness Statements

Tina Lopez: “Heard shouting. Thought it was the boyfriend.”

Ron Dillon: “Saw a man in a hoodie leaving around 11. Not Devon.”

Elise Tran: “Timeline never fit. Devon was seen on traffic cam at 10:55.”

Evidence Collected

Anonymous Letter: “You got the wrong man.”

Surveillance Footage: Blurry image of hooded man entering Unit 4B.

Voicemail: “He said he’d kill me if I told.”
