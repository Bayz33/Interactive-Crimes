Case 1: The Ember Lounge Arson

On June 1st, just after closing, The Ember Lounge went up in flames. By the time firefighters broke in, they found only ash and the burned remains of Marcus Kent, the bar's manager.

Arson investigators called the fire suspicious. Five people were at the bar that night. One of them knows the truth.

Your mission: Solve the case by answering three critical questions:

  • Who started the fire?
  • What was their motive?
  • What key piece of evidence proves it?
