Her husband said she went to visit her sister. But no one ever saw her again. You’ve been called to reopen the file—and something smells off.

Your mission: Solve the case by answering three critical questions:

  • Who is responsible for her disappearance?
  • What was their motive?
  • What key piece of evidence proves it?

Crime Scene Report

Location: 223 Hawthorn Drive – quiet suburban neighborhood.

Last Seen: June 4th, 8:30pm by the neighbor across the street.

Notable Findings: Blood traces under living room rug. Cell phone wiped clean. Husband’s alibi inconsistent.

Witness Statements

Neighbor (Eileen Jacobs): “I saw her arguing with her husband on the porch that night. She looked scared.”

Sister (Karen Moore): “She never showed up. I thought she changed her mind. Now I’m not so sure...”

Husband (Craig Lawson): “She left after dinner. We weren’t arguing, that’s ridiculous.”

Evidence Locker

Rug Blood Samples: Matched to missing wife. Husband claims nosebleed. Amount inconsistent with explanation.

Security Camera: Back door camera mysteriously turned off 30 mins before disappearance.

Text Log: Final message to sister says, “He’s getting worse. If I don’t call—look for me.”
