Case 3: The Vanished Wife

FREE for a limited time

On a quiet autumn evening, a woman vanished from her home with no signs of struggle. No forced entry. No known enemies. Just a mystery—and one confused husband.

Your mission: Solve the case by answering three critical questions:

  • Who is responsible for her disappearance?
  • What was their motive?
  • What key piece of evidence proves it?

Witness Statements

Husband (Daniel Moore): “We were arguing about her sister’s accusations. I went for a walk to cool off. When I came back, she was gone.”

Sister (Lana Harris): “He was controlling. She told me that she felt unsafe. I should’ve done something sooner.”

Neighbor: “I saw someone leaving their house that night—wearing a hoodie. It didn’t look like Daniel.”

Evidence Collected

Security Footage: Shows a hooded figure leaving through the back gate at 10:43 PM.

Phone Records: Multiple calls between Lana and the missing woman leading up to her disappearance.

Journal Entry: “If anything happens to me, look at Lana.”
